# Panning Theory
## Calculating speaker gain, given a desired perceptual angle of a sound and angle of the speakers to the listener:
In this interactive graph, the listeners head is at the origin facing the +x axis.

Drag θ to change the angle of the speakers. 

Drag φ to change the desired percieved angle of the sound. 

The y position of the green and blue x's, to the left of the origin, represent how much gain to apply to each speaker to achieve the desired φ.
